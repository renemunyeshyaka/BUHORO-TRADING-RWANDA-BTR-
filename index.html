About

Buhoro Trading Rwanda (BTR) Ltd

Location: Nyarugenge - Kigali - Rwanda, near Kigali city market, KN 76st

Buhoro Trading Rwanda (BTR) LTD is a company which is specialised in imports and exports of high-quality edible, non-alcoholic beverage and hygienic products. We are dedicated to bringing unique flavors and experiences to our customers, and we work closely with our suppliers to ensure that our products are of the highest quality.

Our range of products includes Rice, Sugar, Sunflower oil, non-alcoholic beverages and hygienic products.
